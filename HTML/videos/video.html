VIDEO PLAYER

Syntax :- The video tag is used to embed video content in a document.

Subject :-

* The video tag contains one or more source tag with different video sources.

* The browser will choose the first source it supports.

* The text betwen the video tag will only be displayed in browers that do not support the video element.

* There are three supported video formats in HTML :- Mp4, WebM, and OGG.
